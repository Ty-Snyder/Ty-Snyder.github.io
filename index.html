Engineering Core Principles

I actively seek out projects that push me out of my comfort zone, but I do so strategically. My approach is to find a balance between the known and the unknown using skills I’ve mastered to anchor myself while diving headfirst into completely new challenges. This allows me to tackle complex problems without being overwhelmed.

Above all, I measure success by growth. If I look back at a project I finished six months ago and can’t find ten things I would do differently, I consider that a failure. To me, engineering isn't about building the perfect machine once. It's about ensuring the next one is always better.
